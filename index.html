JOSAN GONZALEZ

Artist and Author of The Future Is Now

About

Josan Gonzalez is an ilustrator from Barcelona, Spain, known on social media as DeathBurguer. He is the name behind the cyberpunk graphic novel series: The Future is Now, set in a dystopian cyberpunk world dominated by technology.

The art book comes in two volumes, both of which have already been published and distributed by the artist through Kickstarter, a platform used by the community to financially contribute to his work. The book is filled with easter eggs and all the elements known to make up the cyberpunk atmosphere: the high tech low life premise, the intrincate look of urban scenarios, the power hunger corporations, the nonstop surveillance from the government over its citizens, the police brutality, the robotic body augmentation and many other themes explored by the artist.

From a technical point of view, his art is incredibly detailed even though he uses flat applications of color without any shading. The intricacy of his drawings can be seen primarily through the texture details and the highly amount of elements that make up the scenes. In a cyberpunk world the frontiers that separates one thing from another is blurred by the advancement of technology. Gonzalez makes sure to depict this idea very well by connecting everything and everyone with pieces of electronic devices and electric cables, which is why you won't see any character, especially a human one, not using some sort of robotic augmentation.

Gonzalez has also made different artworks for Medium Corp, Editora Aleph, Wired Magazine, Paramount Pictures, Z2 Comics, Harmonix Systems, Metallica, Mondo, Pearl Jam, CDProjekt Red, Epic Games, Riot Games, Dungeons & Dragons and Wizards of The Coast.
